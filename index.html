How to Play Ultimate Jumping Adventure

🎯 Objective

Jump over obstacles to score points and survive as long as possible. The game gets faster and more challenging as your score increases!

🎮 Controls

SPACEBAR
Make your character jump over obstacles
P
Pause/Resume the game
R
Restart the game

⚡ Power-ups

Shield

Protects you from one collision

Speed Boost

Temporarily slow down obstacles

Super Jump

Jump higher for a few seconds

🏆 Scoring & Levels

  • Base Score: +10 points per obstacle cleared
  • Level Bonus: Score multiplier increases with level
  • Perfect Jump: +5 bonus for near-miss jumps
  • Speed Levels: Game speed increases every 100 points
  • Achievement Rewards: Unlock special bonuses
